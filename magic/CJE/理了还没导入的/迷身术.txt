犠性者を魅了する

迷身术
消耗：2点魔法值；1D6点SAN值
施法用时：1轮
施法者在法术生效之前必须能和目标平静地交谈。在一轮左右的交谈之后，施法者须和目标进行一次对抗检定并胜出，法术才能生效。若成功，目标会被定住无法动弹，呆若木鸡，只有物理攻击或者类似的惊吓事件能使其从恍惚状态中恢复过来。
别名：麻木定身、诱骗的银舌

ENTHRALLVICTIM/EnthrallVictim
Cost: 2 magic points; 1D6 Sanity points
Casting time: 1 round
The caster must be able to speak calmly with the target before the spell can take effect. After a round or so of talking, an opposed POW roll is made between the caster and target: if the caster is successful, the target stands struck, numb and dumb, until relieved from the trance by physical assault or some similarly shocking event.
Alternative names: Strike Dumb, The Silver Beguiling Tongue.
